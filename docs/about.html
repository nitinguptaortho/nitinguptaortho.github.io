About Dr. Nitin Gupta

With a deep-rooted commitment to excellence and a passion for advancing orthopaedic care, Dr. Nitin Gupta has steadily emerged as a paragon in joint replacement and Pelviacetabular surgeons. A maestro in his field, he boasts of a remarkable track record, successfully performing over 3,000 joint replacement surgeries, earning accolades and respect from his peers and patients alike.

About Bone and Joint Clinic

Nestled in the heart of Jaipur, the Bone and Joint Clinic stands as a testament to advanced orthopaedic care and unwavering dedication to patient wellness. Spearheaded by the renowned Dr. Nitin Gupta, the clinic thrives on cutting-edge techniques, state-of-the-art facilities, and a personalised approach to every patient's needs.

  • Team of Experts: Apart from Dr. Nitin, the clinic boasts of a seasoned team of joint specialists, ligament experts, and other dedicated medical professionals ensuring optimal care.

  • Reputation: Recognized for its excellence, the clinic has been acclaimed as a haven for those seeking the best bone doctor in Jaipur and the expertise of the best knee replacement surgeon in the city.
