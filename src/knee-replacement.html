Why Knee Replacement Surgery?

Osteoarthritis is the most common reason people opt for knee replacement surgery. It's a degenerative joint disease that wears away the joint cartilage, leading to painful bone-on-bone friction. But other conditions, such as rheumatoid arthritis or traumatic injuries, can also warrant this surgery.

How Does Knee Replacement Surgery Work?

A skilled knee specialist in Jaipur would explain that the surgery involves replacing the damaged knee joint with an artificial joint made of metal and plastic components. The primary goal is to provide a smooth, frictionless surface, restoring the knee's function and relieving pain.

Benefits of Knee Replacement Surgery

1. Pain Relief: For many, the surgery offers significant or complete pain relief.
2. Enhanced Mobility: Post-surgery, patients typically experience improved range of motion and can resume activities that were previously hindered.
3. Improved Quality of Life: Without the constant pain and mobility constraints, individuals can lead more active, fulfilling lives.

Post-Surgery Recovery

Recovery is a gradual process. Initially, you might need aids like crutches or walkers. Physical therapy is crucial, helping regain strength and mobility. Regular check-ups with the knee specialist in Jaipur who performed the surgery will ensure that the healing is on track.
